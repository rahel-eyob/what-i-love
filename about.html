about music

Every beat, every lyric, every harmony speaks to me in its own language.
Music has the power to inspire, heal, and transport me to worlds I've never been.
I embrace every sound that makes my heart move.

Here are some of my favorite artist

yema

Yema is one of the most inspiring new Ethiopian musicians.
Her voice is rich with emotion, mixing modern sound with Ethiopian culture and rhythm.
Her songs speak about love, hope, and everyday life — and every track feels honest and beautiful.

Gossaye Tessfaye

Gossaye Tesfaye born April 1975 is a prominent Ethiopian singer and songwriter known for his high tenor voice and blend of traditional Ethiopian melodies with modern arrangements.
He is considered a leading figure in contemporary Ethiopian pop music

Abeba Dessalegn

Abeba Desalegn is my number one female singer — the best, in my heart.
Her music is different, deep, and full of meaning. She sings about love, hard work, faith, and the strength of women, inspiring girls to believe in themselves.
Every song she creates feels honest and powerful, carrying messages that stay with you long after the music ends.

Teddy Afro

Tewodros Kassahun Germamo born July 14, 1976, known professionally as Teddy Afro,
he is a legendary Ethiopian singer-songwriter considered one of the most influential cultural figures in modern Ethiopian history.
His music blends reggae fusion with traditional Ethiopian styles, often carrying profound social and political messages.
